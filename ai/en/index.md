# Account Administrator Manual

This manual is intended for **account administrators** — training managers, HR coordinators, test coordinators — and describes step by step the common operations on the administration console of the Isograd platform.

Screenshots are generated automatically from the latest versions of the code and therefore remain faithful to what you see on screen.

## Account and Administration

- [Account management](account/) — account details, packs and credits, credit alerts.
- [Administrator management](admins/) — add, edit, grant privileges, disable administrators on your account.

## Candidates and Test Sessions

- [Candidate management](candidates/) — add, import, register to tests, invite by email, organize into groups.
- [Test session management](sessions/) — create and schedule sessions, bulk import, manage the exam calendar.

## Result Tracking

- [Results management](results/) — view test attempts, download reports and certificates, grade manually-marked tests.
- [Test proctoring](proctoring/) — configure proctoring profiles, validate or invalidate proctored attempts.

## Communication

- [Email management](mail-templates/) — message templates, verified senders, custom banner, scheduled sendings.

## Configuration

- [Default options](default-options/) — test settings common to evaluations and certifications.
- [Skill profiles](jobs/) — reference frameworks for the skills expected per job role.
